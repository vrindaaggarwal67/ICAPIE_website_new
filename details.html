Greetings from ICAPIE 2021

6th International Conference on Advanced Production and Industrial Engineering (ICAPIE 2021) is being organized by CAPIER DTU during 18-19 June, 2021 at Delhi. The International Conference ICAPIE 2021, provides a forum to the best talents from the academia & research institutions, industry experts, designers, management professionals, engineers and executives to collaborate and address the current challenges, in order to explore new windows for discovery, innovation and advancements, promoting the contribution of Production and Industrial Engineering & Technology for a sustainable future. The conference is also aimed to promote synergetic partnership between academia and industry and also to show case the growth of cutting edge technology developed in the field of Production and Industrial Engineering in recent past. All full paper submissions will be peer-reviewed and evaluated based on originality, technical and/or research content/depth, correctness, relevance to conference, contributions, and readability.
